Home Sketchub

Sketchub is one of my best project that I made through android app Sketchware, I tried to make it in my free time so thats why it took me many months to build complete, btw it is not complete at all because of shortage of time.
Through this app, one can upload his sketchware creation to the community. It have lot of minor features that make it cool. When Sketchware Officially closed its support for sharing projects due to some google T&C , this app came in huge demand. It was the first app to share sketchware projects.

    Features
  • Upload Apk and Project
  • Like and comment
  • Deep Linking to share posts
  • Share projects privately
  • Share projects with password encryption
  • Chatting (Removed)
  • Administrative Panel
  • Various Badges for users

There are lot of unlisted things but this was great fun to make this project.
You can download this app from the website www.sketchub.cf. Thanks for reading.
